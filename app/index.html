Your Licence Number

Your licence number is a unique number which was allocated to you when you first applied for your Irish driving licence.

This number will always appear on any Irish driving licence you hold.

The location of the driver number on your licence depends on when the licence was issued. To avoid confusion, please note that your driver number is not the number printed in red on your driver licence. The driver number has up to 9 digits (not including spaces) and the format of these digits depends on when the licence was issued. Here are some examples of how the driver licence appears: 28/0800111 or 209 080 111 or 29/0800111.
